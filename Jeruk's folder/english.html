The National Video Game Museum

The National Videogame Museum is the place to be for anyone who wants to know more about the history, social and cultural cultural side of video games. The museum also lets its visitors experience the games of tomorrow. Gamers from young to old are welcome to play, discover, learn and share experiences online.

Our history

Here is our story

Two friends started a collection of arcade video games in a garage box in 2008 and gradually moved to bigger locations as the collection expanded. They eventually turned it into a professional business and started organizing events for the community. They were approached by a city marketer who suggested turning it into a museum, which led to the creation of the National Videogame Museum in Zoetermeer. The museum has been a huge success and they are now working on expanding it and turning it into a total concept including a National Game Knowledge Center, National Video Game Archive, eSports arena, Hacker/makerspace, game fair and event venue.
